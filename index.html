Audio samples from "BemaGAN: Generative Adversarial Networks with Multi Envelope Discriminator"

Abstract: This paper introduces BemaGAN (Bespoke Envelope Multi-Discriminator Adaptive GAN), a novel adaptation of the HiFi-GAN architecture enhanced with a Multi-Envelope Discriminator (MED) to improve audio periodicity sensitivity, fidelity, and perceptual quality. We have further developed Audim, a new audio generative AI model, by applying BemaGAN to the vocoder of an existing audio AI model. Our research utilizes Audim to advance personalized digital treatment technologies for tinnitus, specifically through an improved TMNMT (Tailored Music Notched Music Therapy) approach. This method, which adapts music to individual tinnitus frequencies and personal genre preferences, traditionally faced challenges with limited music selection and prolonged treatment durations. By integrating Audim, we enhance audio quality and variety, effectively increasing patient engagement and treatment effectiveness. Preliminary tests indicate that Audim performs robustly across both objective and subjective metrics, promising significant advancements in tinnitus treatment environments.
